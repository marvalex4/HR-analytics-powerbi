# HR Analytics Dashboard - Power BI

## Overview
An interactive HR Analytics dashboard built in Power BI to analyze 
employee attrition, workforce demographics, and compensation patterns 
across departments and job roles at IBM.

## Dashboard Pages
1. **HR Overview** : High level KPIs including total employees, 
   attrition count, attrition rate and average monthly income
2. **Attrition Analysis** : Deep dive into attrition by job role, 
   age group and gender
3. **Employee Demographics** : Workforce breakdown by department, 
   job role, education level, gender and business travel frequency
4. **Compensation Analysis** : Income comparison by job role, 
   department, age group and attrition status

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is 16.12%
- Sales Representatives have the highest attrition rate
- Employees under 25 are most likely to leave
- Employees who left earned significantly less than those who stayed
- Managers and Research Directors earn the highest monthly income
- Research & Development is the largest department

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

## DAX Measures Created
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Avg Monthly Income
- Avg Age

## Dataset
- WA-Fn-UseC-HR-Employee-Attrition.csv (sourced from Kaggle)
- 1,470 employee records across 36 columns
